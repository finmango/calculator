Building a Portfolio

In order to invest your savings, you need to build a portfolio. The portfolio consists of a series of assets to which funds will be allocated. Those assets can be stocks, bonds, and other financial instruments. Which assets are selected and the proportion of funds allocated to them is what makes a it portfolio.

There's no one-size-fits-all strategy when building a portfolio, but there are several formulas that have consistently worked for a number of investment objectives. At FinMango, we are strong believers of long-term strategies based on low-cost, broad-market indices. By investing in broad-market indices, your wealth will be correlated to the growth of the overall economy, instead of depending on the success of individual companies.

We have collected financial data dating back to 1970 and built this portfolio simulator, so you can see how different portfolios and strategies would have performed in a variety of economic conditions.
